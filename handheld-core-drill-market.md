<p><strong>The Handheld Core Drill Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Core Drill</li><li>Wet Core Drill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld core drills are categorized into two main types: dry core drills and wet core drills. Dry core drills operate without water, utilizing specialized bits to extract concrete or masonry samples, making them ideal for quick, on-site applications. Conversely, wet core drills require water to cool the drill bit and remove debris, optimizing performance and extending bit life. Both types cater to different project needs, with dry drills excelling in portability and wet drills ensuring efficient cutting in tougher materials.</p></p>
