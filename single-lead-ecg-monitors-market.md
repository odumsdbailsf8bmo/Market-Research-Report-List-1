<p><p>The Single-Lead ECG Monitors market comprises two primary types: Recording-Based Single-Lead ECG Monitors and Real-Time Single-Lead ECG Monitors. Recording-Based devices capture and store ECG data for later analysis, providing a useful option for remote patient monitoring and offline diagnosis.
